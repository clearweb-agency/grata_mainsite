Grata’s Smart Lock Product

Bluetooth and Wifi enabled allowing for easy entrance for any resident using the Grata App. Part of a digital smart lock ecosystem in multi-family buildings giving residents’ a “master-key” for multiple points of access.

Our smart locks are installed on all common areas and suites  and are all accessible through the Grata App.

Grata Leadership Team

Ken Crema

Co-Founder

Ken has been a serial entrepreneur since his first venture at the age of 17. He has successfully founded and exited multiple companies including EDM (Electronic Direct Marketing), MCCI, and 2C2S, and he is now a Founder and Partner of S&P Data. Ken sits on the board and is an advisor to a few private companies today. Ken had the privilege of being nominated into the Young Presidents’ Organization in his youth and his company, MCCI, was recognized as the fastest growing company in Canada by Profit Magazine in 2005.

Wayne Jacobs

Co-Founder

Wayne, originally from South Africa spent a decade in New York City & London, England before moving to Toronto in the late 1980’s. He was 1 of 2 founding Partners of Allied Properties a TSX Listed REIT. He exited the company after 24 years. Following this he spent most of his time investing for his own account before Co-Founding Grata Technologies Inc. Wayne has a passion for spending time in the African bush.

Troy Crema

Co-Founder

Troy has been an entrepreneur-in-residence since graduating from Dartmouth College in 2017. Troy has consulted multiple startups in the tech space and has worked with prominent incubators and family offices in North America. Troy studied economics and theatre at Dartmouth College, while also playing for the Men’s Ice Hockey team. Troy was nominated for the Hobey Baker award, and was named to First Team All-Ivy in his final season for his performance on the ice and in the classroom. Troy went on to play professional hockey for the Buffalo Sabres and Toronto Maple Leafs organizations before focusing on his entrepreneurial endeavours.
